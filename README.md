### Vehicle Damage Detection & Alteration System *(Final Year Project)*

YOLOv8 paired with an MLX90614 infrared sensor on Raspberry Pi for automated vehicle damage assessment and fraud detection. The system flags damage regions, cross-references thermal anomalies for hidden repairs, and generates PDF reports for insurance and forensic use. Currently in field testing with domain validators.
